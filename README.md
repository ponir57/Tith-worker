# Tith-Worker

> Your silent co-worker. Always active. Always on.

Tith-Worker is a free, open-source activity simulation tool 
built for remote workers, freelancers, and WFH professionals 
who rely on time tracking software to log their productivity.

Whether you're on a quick coffee break, stuck in a meeting, 
or just stepping away from your desk — Tith-Worker keeps your 
system active with realistic mouse movements and keyboard 
activity, so your tracker never marks you as idle.

No more anxiety about "going offline." No more interrupted 
sessions. Just peace of mind, one click away.

## ✨ What Makes It Different?

Most activity tools are bloated, paid, or just plain sketchy. 
Tith-Worker is none of that.

- 🖱️ **Smart Mouse Simulation** — natural, randomized movement 
  patterns that look and feel human
- ⌨️ **Keyboard Activity** — subtle keystrokes that keep your 
  session alive without interfering with your work
- 🔘 **One-Click Toggle** — turn it ON or OFF in seconds, 
  no technical knowledge needed
- 🪶 **Ultra Lightweight** — runs quietly in the background 
  without slowing down your machine
- 🐍 **Pure Python** — cross-platform, easy to install, 
  easy to understand
- 🔓 **100% Free & Open Source** — always free, forever

## 🚀 Get Started in 30 Seconds

git clone https://github.com/yourusername/tith-worker
cd tith-worker
pip install -r requirements.txt
python main.py

That's it. No complicated setup. No license keys. 
No hidden fees. Just run it and get back to your life.
